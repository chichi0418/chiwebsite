Taipei Municipal Jianguo Senior High School

Jianguo Middle School is a famous senior high school located in Taipei, Taiwan. It was founded in 1946. It was originally a school for the children of veterans, with the purpose of taking care of the education of the children of the National Army officers and soldiers. The school is located in Daan District, Taipei City, adjacent to Daan Forest Park, with beautiful environment and convenient transportation.

Jianguo Middle School is known for its strict academic standards and comprehensive education system, offering courses in natural sciences, social sciences, language arts, etc. In addition, the school attaches great importance to the all-round development of students, not only with excellent academic performance, but also with considerable performance and achievements in sports, music and other artistic fields.

Jianguo Middle School has complete campus facilities, including modern classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and multi-functional gymnasiums. The school also attaches great importance to international exchanges and has exchanges and cooperation with schools in many countries to provide students with overseas study and exchange opportunities.

In addition, most graduates of Jianguo Middle School can enter top universities in Taiwan and the world for further studies. The school has many famous alumni among its alumni, including leaders in academia, politics, business and other fields.
